System High

UX/UI Design

Role Senior Designer
Link systemhigh.com

I led the redesign of the System High website, transforming it into a modern, clean, and user-friendly interface within a tight two-week deadline. Using Figma, I crafted a streamlined design that enabled our WordPress developer to efficiently complete the build the following week. To maintain brand integrity, I creatively incorporated elements of the System High logo into the homepage banner, ensuring the brand’s strong identity remained front and center throughout the site. I also delivered fully responsive tablet and mobile designs, which the client approved, providing a seamless experience across all devices.
