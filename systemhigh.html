System High

UX/UI Design

Role Senior Designer
Link systemhigh.com

I was tasked to redesign the System High website into a modern and clean interface in just two weeks. I created a simple design on Figma that allowed the Wordpress developer on the team to finish the project within the next week. For the design, I wanted to play with the System High logo and bring that into the Homepage banner image. They had a strong brand and I didn't want that to be lost throughout the design. I also delivered Table and Mobile views for the client to approve.
