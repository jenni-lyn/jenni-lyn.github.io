No Kid Hungry

UX/UI Design

Role Senior Designer
Link nokidhungry.org/rebuild

I designed a microsite for No Kid Hungry’s Fall Campaign—an initiative that used murals to raise awareness and inspire action around the organization’s mission. The goal was to showcase all five murals on a single, cohesive page. To keep the focus on the artwork, I opted for a clean, minimalist design that made navigation intuitive. The murals are prominently featured in the header, allowing users to easily jump between each one without getting lost in the broader branding.
