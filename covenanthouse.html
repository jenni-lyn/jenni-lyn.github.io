I designed the 2020 Annual Report for Covenant House Greater Washington. The colors and typography I chose was very much inspired by the organization's logo. I wanted the report to engaging to encourage readers to keep turning the page. I used a number of colors to complement the blue color of the logo. It was important to include iconography and images throughout the report to break up the text and focus on the people.
