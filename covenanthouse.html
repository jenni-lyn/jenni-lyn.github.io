I designed the 2020 Annual Report for Covenant House Greater Washington, drawing inspiration from the organization’s logo to create a cohesive visual identity. I carefully selected colors and typography that complemented the logo’s blue while adding vibrant accents to keep the design lively and inviting. My goal was to craft an engaging report that encourages readers to keep turning the pages. To enhance readability and emphasize the people at the heart of the organization, I incorporated thoughtful iconography and compelling imagery throughout, effectively breaking up the text and bringing the story to life.
